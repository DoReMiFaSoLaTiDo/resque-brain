Killing a Worker

It is unsafe to kill a worker from Resque-Brain. See here for an explanation. To do it yourself, you must:

  1. Get a production console to {{resqueSelected.name}}
  2. Find the worker instance and unregister it: 
    worker = Resque.workers.detect { |worker|
  worker.id == '{{job.worker}}'
}
worker.unregister_worker
